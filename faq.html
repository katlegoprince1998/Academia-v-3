FAQs

I see online applications, what is it?

The Online Applications Portal is a portal that helps making applying/enrolling your child to our school much more convinient. The portal removes the hardcopy school applications form and replaces it with your mobile handset to enroll your child at our school.

What is the age for enrollment?

The Department of Education requires chidren enrolling at Primary Schools to be
Ages 5-18 years old.
Grade R - 5 years and Turning 6 Years in June
Grade 1: 7 Years | Grade 2: 8 Years
Grade 3: 9 Years | Grade 4: 10 Years
Grade 5: 11 Years | Grade 6: 12 Years
Grade 7: 13 Years | Grade 8: 14 Years
Grade 9: 15 Years | Grade 10: 16 Years
Grade 11: 17 Years | Grade 12: 18 Years

What other languages are taught?

The school offers two languages as part of the curriculum,IsiXhosa Home Language, English Home Language and Afrikaans First Additional Language.

What curriculum is followed at the school?

As a School that is registered with the Western Cape Department of Education we are required to follow the CAPS curriculum.
